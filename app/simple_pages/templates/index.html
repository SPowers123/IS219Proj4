Welcome

This is a simple web app that allows users to post and edit their recipes and view the recipes of other users. It was made for my IS219 class, which is a web development class focused on building Flask applications.

To use the application, you must first register with an email and a password. You will then be directed to a form that allows you to add new recipes while editing or deleting any you have already made. A recipe has a title and a text area to put in content. Titles do not have to be unique.
